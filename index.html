Philosophy
The Indiraé Ethos

We do not repeat. We do not replicate. Each Indiraé interior is a singular dialogue between architecture, light, and the soul of its dweller. Founded by fourth-generation design custodians, our atelier fuses old-world savoir-faire with futurist minimalism.

— Indiraé Living, est. 2024

Bespoke Spatial Curation

"Tailoring the physical layout and flow of your home to match the rhythm of your lifestyle, ensuring every square inch serves a purpose."
We believe that true luxury begins with how a space moves. Every corridor, transition, and sightline is choreographed to enhance the way you live, entertain, and rest. Our holistic approach transforms raw square footage into a seamless narrative of everyday life.
  • Custom Furniture & Millwork Design
  • Fine Art & Antique Advisory
  • Tailored Furniture Layouts & Flow Mapping

Sensory & Atmospheric Design

"Crafting the invisible elements of luxury—how a room reacts to morning light, the acoustics of a high ceiling, and the tactile richness of premium materials."
Luxury is felt, not just seen. We engineer the intangible — the warmth of golden-hour light, the silence of a perfectly acoustical space, the irresistible touch of hand-selected textures. Every surface, shadow, and sound is meticulously curated to evoke emotion.
  • Architectural Lighting & Daylighting Schemes
  • Acoustic Softening & Soundscaping
  • Tactile Material & Textile Curation

Architectural Evolution

"Bridging the gap between the historic structural bones of a property and modern, future-proof innovation without losing character."
We respect the past while engineering for the future. Whether preserving centuries-old havelis or reimagining modern penthouses, we honor soul while integrating invisible intelligence. Tradition and technology coexist in perfect harmony.
  • Intelligent Smart-Home Integration
  • Heritage & Period Property Restoration
  • Interior Architectural Remodeling

Turnkey Lifestyle Integration

"An entirely white-glove, stress-free experience. We handle the chaos of construction so you only experience the joy of the reveal."
From blueprint to breakfast-in-bed, we manage every detail. Our single-point accountability means zero stress for you — only the pleasure of walking into a home that is completely, effortlessly yours. Your life, uninterrupted; your home, delivered.
  • Full-Scale Project Management & Procurement
  • Vetted Contractor & Artisan Liaison
  • Concierge Styling (Ready-to-Live Move-In)
